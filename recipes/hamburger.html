Oreo Cheeseburger Recipe (Serves 2–3)

Sweet? Savory? Surreal? This burger blends rich beef, melted cheese, and Oreos into a wild culinary experiment.

Ingredients

  • 600g ground beef (80/20 mix recommended)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 egg (optional, for binding)
  • 4 slices of melting cheese (e.g. American or mozzarella)
  • 12 Oreo cookies (plus extra for garnish)
  • 3 sesame seed burger buns
  • Butter or oil for frying
  • Optional: a splash of heavy cream for a cookie-infused cheese sauce

Instructions

  1. Make the patties

    Season the ground beef with salt and pepper. Add the egg if desired for a firmer patty. Form into 3–4 thick patties.

  2. Cook the burgers

    In a hot, lightly oiled pan or grill, cook patties for 4–5 minutes per side until well browned and cooked through. Add cheese slices during the last minute of cooking.

  3. Toast the buns

    Split the buns and lightly toast the insides in the same pan or grill until golden.

  4. Prepare the cookie layer

    Twist apart the Oreos and layer the cream side directly on top of the hot cheese, or use whole Oreos between patties for extra crunch. For a more daring touch, melt a few crushed Oreos with cream and drizzle over the patties as a sweet-savory sauce.

  5. Assemble the burger

    Layer your burger like this:

    • Bottom bun
    • Burger patty with cheese
    • Oreos (whole or halves)
    • Another burger patty + cheese
    • More Oreos
    • Top bun

    Repeat the stack if you're going for a monster build, as seen in the image.

  6. Serve

    Serve immediately while the cheese is gooey. Expect strange looks and possibly applause.

This one's not for the faint of heart—or for purists. But it’s certainly unforgettable.
