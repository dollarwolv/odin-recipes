Easy Lasagna Recipe (Serves 4–6)

Doesn't this make your mouth water? To see how to make this amazing lasagna, follow along?

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 250g ricotta or cottage cheese
  • 200g shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Optional: a handful of fresh spinach or chopped parsley for extra greens

Instructions

  1. Cook the noodles

    Boil the lasagna noodles in salted water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Make the sauces

    In a large pan, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Sauté the chopped onion until soft, then add garlic. Add the ground meat and cook until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer for 10–15 minutes. Add a bit of water if it gets too thick.

  3. Preheat your oven

    Set it to 180°C (350°F).

  4. Assemble the lasagna

    In a baking dish, start with a thin layer of meat sauce. Then layer:

    • 3 noodles
    • Half of the ricotta
    • A third of the meat sauce
    • A handful of mozzarella

    Repeat once more. Finish with the last 3 noodles, the remaining sauce, and top with mozzarella and Parmesan.

  5. Bake

    Cover with foil (lightly oil the foil so it doesn’t stick to the cheese) and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 15 minutes until golden and bubbling.

  6. Rest and serve

    Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing. This helps it set.

Now you're done! Hope you enjoy your Lasagna.
